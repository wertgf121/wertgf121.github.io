EPISODE IV
A NEW HOPE FOR CAMA CONVERSION

It is a time of great change at the Tarrant Appraisal District.

The fearless PS2 has been entrusted with a critical mission: to bring to life a legendary animation that will inspire the galaxy.

Yet confusion stirs in the ranks — whispers of PS1 and PS2 clash, a rivalry born of similar names but distinct destinies.

In the shadows, SWO, the Jedi Master of APIs, commands unseen forces to shape the flow of data.

Allan, the conqueror of the elusive Transparency Stars, charts the course through the vast, unseen realms of clarity.

Don, the silent webmaster, weaves the web that holds the network together, while Nathan and Blake stand as vigilant sentinels, guardians of security’s gate.

Behind the scenes, Johnathan, the master architect, constructs the digital foundations upon which all depends.

Greg and Roby, twin engineers of the infrastructure fleet, fortify the system against chaos and decay.

Donna, Avery, and Tiffany G, cartographers of the GIS cosmos, map the uncharted territories with precision and care.

Tiffany H patrols the PC and network nebula, ensuring seamless connection across the expanse.

Robin, the guardian of quality, ensures every mission meets its highest standard.

High above it all, Brian leads the Information Systems forces, with Michael by his side, ready to execute the grand design.

As the countdown begins, the fate of CAMA hangs in the balance, and the Tarrant Appraisal District prepares to embrace a new dawn...
